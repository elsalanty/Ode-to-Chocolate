An Ode to Chocolate

A Chocolatey Reflection

I love chocolate sooooo much. If I could I would eat chocolate every day. every single day, every single hour, every single second. I am so obsessed about chocolate that I dream about it every single night. People tell me I have a problem, but I say, "How can chocolate be a problem? Chocolate is the answer."
